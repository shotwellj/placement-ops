8-Dimension Scoring

Technical match, seniority fit, location, comp alignment, culture, gap analysis, presentation risk, and fill probability — all weighted and scored.

◬

Predictive Forecasting

Hiring signal detection, expansion prediction, and fill probability modeling. Know who's hiring before they post the job.

Comp hygiene analysis, posting quality audits, time-to-fill benchmarks, and talent flow mapping across industries.

Strategy Reports

Client-facing talent strategy with org charts, hiring sequences, comp benchmarking, risk assessments, and fee projections.
